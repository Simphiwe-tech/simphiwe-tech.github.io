CAPACITATE CO-LAB

The format of the CAPACITATE Co-Lab will be virtual starting with an Interactive webinar focusing on three areas:
sector trends, challenges and opportunities in the South African energy sector

Free

.

16:00 to 17:30 SAST on 30 March 2023

• Connect with businesses in South Africa and the US
• Build international partnerships

Who can participate?
•Registered businesses operating over 12 months
• Annual turnover less than R5 million
• Majority black woman-owned
