CAPACITATE CO-LAB

The CAPACITATE Co-Lab session aims to develop energy value chains and strengthen industry partnerships between US and SA businesses. 30 South African women-owned businesses will be connected with 30 US businesses operating in the energy value chain. The CAPACITATE Co-Lab will start with an information sharing session discussing trends, challenges and opportunities in the South African energy sector and will then be followed by networking sessions in small groups enabling participants to foster connections and build global partnerships.

.
It will be held on ZOOM

16:00 to 17:30 SAST on 30 March 2023
